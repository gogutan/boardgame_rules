ITO
⚙ Cooperative 👥 2–10 👤 8+ ⏳ 30 min
What kind of game?

In this cooperative game, each player holds a hidden number from 1 to 100. As a team, place your cards in ascending order — but you can't say your number. Describe it through a shared theme instead.

Listen to your teammates' hints and thread the numbers together:

Line them up — smallest to largest!

Goal

All cards end up in ascending order from 1 to 100.

How to play
1
Setup. Deal 1 number card (1–100) face-down to each player. Only you may look at your own card.
2
Pick a theme. Flip a theme card. As a group, agree on a theme — for example, "animal strength."
3
Give hints. Describe your number through the theme. Never say the number itself.
Example — "animal strength"
Near 1 — "ant"
Near 100 — "elephant"
4
Play in order. Keep talking together. Place cards face-down in ascending order, as best you can guess.
5
Reveal and judge. Once everyone has played, flip the cards over. If they form an ascending 1 → 100 sequence, the team wins.
