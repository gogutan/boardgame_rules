COYOTE
⚙ Bluff 👥 2–10 👤 10+ ⏳ 15–30 min
What kind of game?

In this game, you look at the other players' numbers and try to predict the total sum of everyone's cards. Guess your own card and declare a number that doesn't exceed the total.

If another player's number seems suspicious, take the leap:

Ready, set — "Coyote!!"

Victory condition

Survive to the end without being eliminated (losing 3 times).

How to play
1
Deal cards to foreheads. Shuffle the number cards, deal 1 face-down to each player. Hold it against your forehead facing outward — don't look at the front.
2
Starter declares. The starting player looks at everyone else's cards and declares an integer of 1 or greater — estimate the total and stay below it.
3
Next player chooses.
3-1 Declare a higher number → next player continues at step 3.
3-2 Call "Coyote!" — you think the previous number exceeds the real total.
4
Reveal and judge. Lower all cards face-up and sum them.
Special card effects
×2 — double the total
MAX→0 — treat the highest card as 0
? — draw 1 from the pile and use it instead
4-1 Declared number exceeds the total → caller wins, previous player loses.
4-2 Declared number does not exceed → caller loses, previous player wins.
Loser takes damage and loses a life.
5
Redeal. Discard and redeal from the draw pile. The round's winner is the next starting player.
※ If a Night card (black "0") appeared, reshuffle discards back in first.
6
Repeat 1–5 until one player remains.
